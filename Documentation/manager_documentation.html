Getting Started as a Manager

Welcome to ATTENDO Manager Portal! As a manager, you oversee your team's attendance, approve submissions, and ensure compliance.

Your Responsibilities

  • Review and approve daily attendance submissions from your team
  • Resolve attendance mismatches between web submissions and swipe records
  • Send reminders to team members who haven't submitted their status
  • Generate team reports for upper management
  • Monitor attendance patterns and compliance
  • Use AI insights for predictive attendance management

Login Credentials

  • Username: Your Manager ID (e.g., M001, M002)
  • Default Password: manager123 (change on first login)
Manager Tip: Set up a daily routine to check pending approvals at 10 AM and 3 PM to ensure timely processing.

Manager Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your team's attendance status and pending actions.

Dashboard Widgets

  • Total Team Members: Shows the size of your team
  • Present Today: Number of team members marked present (Office/WFH)
  • Pending Approvals: Attendance submissions awaiting your review
  • Issues & Mismatches: Discrepancies requiring attention

Team Status Table

The main table shows today's attendance status for all team members:

  • Color-coded status badges (Green: Present, Yellow: Pending, Red: Absent)
  • Submission timestamps to track punctuality
  • Quick action buttons for approve/reject
  • Mismatch indicators for vendors with discrepancies

Quick Actions Panel

  • Send Daily Reminders: Notify all non-submitters
  • Holiday Calendar: View upcoming holidays
  • Weekly Report: Generate team performance report
  • Review Mismatches: Address all pending discrepancies
  • AI Predictions: View attendance forecasts

Team Management

Effectively manage and monitor your team's attendance patterns.

Team Overview Features

  1. Access your dashboard to see all team members
  2. View real-time status for each vendor
  3. Identify vendors who haven't submitted status (highlighted in yellow)
  4. Check submission times to ensure compliance with 10 AM deadline

Team Size: Managers typically handle teams of 8-15 vendors. If your team grows beyond 20, consider requesting team restructuring.

Approval Management

Review and approve vendor attendance submissions efficiently.

Single Status Approval

  1. Navigate to the "Pending Approvals" section on your dashboard
  2. Review vendor name, date, status, and location
  3. Click the green checkmark to approve
  4. Click the red X to reject (provide reason)
  5. Status updates immediately and vendor is notified

Approval Guidelines

Consider these factors when reviewing submissions:

  • Consistency: Check if status matches expected schedule
  • Location: Verify location is appropriate for status type
  • Patterns: Look for unusual patterns (frequent leaves, etc.)
  • Comments: Read any comments provided by vendor
  • Swipe Data: Cross-reference with biometric records if available

Rejection Process

When rejecting a status:

  1. Click the reject button
  2. Enter a clear reason for rejection
  3. Be specific about what needs correction
  4. Vendor will be notified to resubmit
Important: Always provide a clear rejection reason. Vendors need this feedback to correct their submissions.

Bulk Actions

Process multiple attendance submissions simultaneously to save time.

Bulk Approve All

  1. Review all pending submissions in the table
  2. Select specific items using checkboxes (optional)
  3. Click "Approve All" button
  4. Confirm the bulk action
  5. All selected (or all pending) statuses are approved

Bulk Reject

  1. Select items to reject using checkboxes
  2. Click "Reject All" button
  3. Enter a common rejection reason
  4. Confirm the action
  5. Selected statuses are rejected with the same reason

Send Bulk Reminders

Notify all team members who haven't submitted their status:

  1. Click "Send Daily Reminders" button
  2. System identifies non-submitters automatically
  3. Email reminders are sent to all pending vendors
  4. Confirmation message shows number of reminders sent
Efficiency Tip: Use bulk approve for routine days when most team members are in office. Review individually for Mondays and Fridays when patterns vary.

Mismatch Review

Resolve discrepancies between vendor submissions and swipe machine records.

Understanding Mismatches

Mismatches occur when:

  • Vendor marks present but no swipe record exists
  • Swipe shows present but vendor marked leave/absent
  • Timing discrepancies (late arrival not reflected in status)
  • Location mismatches (office vs actual swipe location)

Review Process

  1. Click "Review Mismatches" from quick actions
  2. Filter by status (Pending/Approved/Rejected) or vendor
  3. Review each mismatch:
    • Date of mismatch
    • Vendor's submitted status
    • Actual swipe record
    • Vendor's explanation
  4. Approve valid explanations (forgot to swipe, machine issues)
  5. Reject invalid explanations with feedback
  6. Add manager comments for record

Common Valid Reasons

  • Swipe machine malfunction
  • Forgot to swipe in/out
  • Client site visit (no swipe access)
  • Emergency situations
  • System downtime
Compliance Note: Unresolved mismatches affect billing accuracy. Aim to resolve all mismatches within 48 hours.

AI Predictions & Insights

Leverage AI-powered predictions to proactively manage attendance.

AI Features

  • Attendance Predictions: 7-day forecast for each team member
  • Risk Assessment: Identify high-risk absence patterns
  • Pattern Recognition: Detect unusual attendance behaviors
  • Recommendation Engine: Suggested actions for improvement

Using AI Insights

  1. Click "AI Predictions" from quick actions
  2. View the prediction dashboard showing:
    • Team attendance forecast graph
    • Individual risk scores
    • Confidence levels for predictions
  3. Filter by prediction window (3, 7, or 14 days)
  4. Click on individual predictions for details
  5. Export predictions for planning purposes

Risk Categories

Low Risk

Regular attendance pattern, minimal concerns

Medium Risk

Some irregularities, monitor closely

High Risk

Significant absence risk, intervention needed

AI Accuracy: Predictions improve over time as more data is collected. Current accuracy rate: ~85% for 7-day forecasts.

Power Automate Notification Management

Receive intelligent, automated notifications through Microsoft Power Automate workflows for efficient team management.

Automated Manager Notifications

ATTENDO's Power Automate integration delivers targeted notifications based on your team's attendance activities, ensuring you stay informed about critical events without information overload.

Smart Notifications You'll Receive

  • Manager Summary Notifications: Automated team status summaries at 12 PM and 2 PM daily
  • Team Completion Alerts: Celebration notification when all team members submit status
  • Mismatch Notifications: Real-time alerts for attendance reconciliation conflicts
  • Late Submission Alerts: Escalated notifications for overdue submissions
  • Monthly Report Notifications: Automated delivery of comprehensive attendance reports
  • System Alerts: Critical system issues requiring your attention

Sending Manual Notifications

  1. Identify team members requiring notification
  2. Click "Send Reminders" button
  3. Select notification type:
    • Status submission reminder
    • Mismatch resolution request
    • General announcement
  4. Customize message (optional)
  5. Send immediately or schedule

Power Automate Notification Flow

How it works: When team events occur, ATTENDO updates Excel notification tables in OneDrive. Power Automate flows monitor these tables every 5-15 minutes, automatically sending relevant notifications to you through Microsoft Teams or email based on the notification type and urgency.

Notification Delivery Channels

  • Microsoft Teams: Primary channel for instant team management updates
  • Email Notifications: Formal notifications with detailed information
  • Dashboard Alerts: In-app notifications when you log in
  • Mobile Push: Teams app notifications for urgent management actions

Intelligent Notification Features

  • Team-Specific Filtering: Only receive notifications for your direct reports
  • Priority-Based Delivery: Critical alerts delivered instantly, summaries on schedule
  • Context-Rich Messages: Include team statistics, completion rates, and actionable insights
  • Auto-Stop Logic: Notifications automatically cease when issues are resolved

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How quickly should I approve vendor submissions?

A: Aim to approve submissions within 24 hours. Daily submissions should be reviewed by end of the same day to maintain accurate records.

Q: Can I modify a vendor's attendance status after approval?

A: No, approved statuses cannot be modified. For corrections, contact the system administrator with proper justification.

Q: What should I do if a vendor repeatedly submits incorrect status?

A: Document the pattern, provide clear feedback through rejections, and if it continues, escalate to HR with evidence.

Q: How do I handle team members in different time zones?

A: The system uses local time zones. Ensure vendors submit based on their local working hours and review accordingly.

Q: Can I delegate approval authority during my absence?

A: Yes, contact the admin to set up a temporary delegate who can approve on your behalf during planned absences.

Q: How accurate are the AI predictions?

A: AI predictions have approximately 85% accuracy for 7-day forecasts. Accuracy improves with more historical data.

Q: What's the difference between rejecting a status and a mismatch explanation?

A: Status rejection means the submitted attendance is incorrect. Mismatch rejection means the explanation for discrepancy is inadequate.

Q: How do I export data for upper management?

A: Use the Reports section to generate comprehensive reports. Select Excel format for data analysis or PDF for presentations.
